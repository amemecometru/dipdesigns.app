the second bridge · mockup → production

Beautiful UIs die without a backend. Yours won't.

Every other tool hands you a gorgeous mockup and leaves you at the cliff. At export, DipDesigns generates a portable backend blueprint for the exact UI you built — data model, endpoints, auth, and a wire-up map binding every control to the endpoint it needs.

Stack-agnostic on principle: we tell you what to build, never lock you into where. Take it to your own infra, your IDE, or hand it straight to Cursor, Claude, or an MCP agent.
